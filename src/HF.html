Pulling from design aesthetics and iconography found in my home, culture, and family, I aim to create heirloom quality furniture in the hopes of one day passing on more than just a legacy to future generations. With a certain humility and intimacy, these pieces of furniture highlight the importance of family and bicultural identity while also being an integral part of daily life.
